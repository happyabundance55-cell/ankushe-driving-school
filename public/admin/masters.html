Class of Vehicle

Vehicle categories used in student records and government forms (Form 14 / Form 15).
Examples: LMV, LMV-TR, MCWG, HMV, HGMV, Transport

DL Issuing Authority

RTO codes for driving licence issuing authorities.
Examples: MH12, MH14, DL01, KA01, TN22

Training Vehicles

Vehicles used for training. The registration number appears in Form 15.
Example: MH12 GK 8618 — Maruti Suzuki Swift
